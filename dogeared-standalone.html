The process

Three steps, no library card

01.

Pick your stack

Browse curated shelves by genre, mood, or theme — or tell us your taste and we'll build your first box for you. Add up to five books.

02.

We ship it free

Your books arrive in a reusable mailer within 3–5 business days. Shipping's free both ways, always.

03.

Read, return, repeat

Keep books for a weekend or a year — there's no due date. Ready for more? Drop the mailer in any mailbox.
